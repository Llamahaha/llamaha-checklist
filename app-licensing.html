Before You Start

Have these details ready first

Account

Your work email

Use the exact work account that should have access to the product. Many licensing issues come from signing in with the wrong account or profile.

Product

The app name and version

Know the product name and, if possible, the version or year shown in the app. This matters most for CAD, PDF, and accounting software.

Message

A screenshot of the error

Capture the exact message if the app says trial, unlicensed, subscription required, activation failed, or sign-in is needed.

Comparison

Browser versus desktop

Check whether the same account works in the vendor portal or browser version. That helps separate account access problems from local app problems.
