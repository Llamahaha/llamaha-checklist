Cheatsheet

Warranty lookup cheatsheet

Where to paste a serial / service tag for each major OEM, what those identifiers look like, and how to find them on the device. Use when scoping a hardware repair or quoting a replacement.

Dell

Service Tag & Express Service Code

Lookup URL

dell.com/support/warranty — enter the Service Tag from Dell Support to get warranty status, ship date, and entitlements.

Service Tag format

7-character alphanumeric (letters + digits, no I/O/L). Example: BX3K7Z2. The Express Service Code is the same tag converted to digits (10–11 numeric).

Where to find it

Sticker on the bottom of laptops or back of desktops/towers. Also visible from CMD: wmic bios get serialnumber or PowerShell: Get-CimInstance Win32_BIOS | Select SerialNumber.

Bulk & API

Dell's TechDirect portal supports CSV upload of multiple Service Tags. API access available via Dell TechDirect for partners.

Lenovo

Serial Number & Machine Type

Lookup URL

pcsupport.lenovo.com — "Detect Product" or paste the Serial Number.

Serial Number format

Typically 7–8 character alphanumeric. Example: PF3ABCDE (ThinkPad) or MP1ABCDE (ThinkCentre). Often paired with a Machine Type Model (MTM) like 20XW for the model line.

Where to find it

Sticker on the bottom of laptops; back of towers. From CMD: wmic bios get serialnumber. Lenovo Vantage or System Update tools also display it.

Bulk & API

Lenovo Support has a multi-serial lookup. Lenovo also offers a Warranty Lookup API to partners; ask your Lenovo rep.

HP

Serial Number & Product Number

Lookup URL

support.hp.com/us-en/checkwarranty — needs Serial Number; sometimes asks for Country/Region.

Serial Number format

10-character alphanumeric. Example: 5CD1234ABC. Product Number (P/N) is separate (e.g., 2K8X5UA#ABA) and identifies the SKU.

Where to find it

Bottom sticker on laptops, back of desktops. From CMD: wmic bios get serialnumber. HP Support Assistant displays both Serial and Product Number.

Bulk & API

HP offers a Warranty Status API for partners; HP Smart Friend Pro / HP Manageability Integration Kit can pull warranty status at scale.

Apple

Serial Number & Coverage

Lookup URL

checkcoverage.apple.com — paste the Serial Number, get hardware + AppleCare status.

Serial Number format

For Macs/iPads/iPhones manufactured 2021 and later, Apple shifted to a randomized 10-character alphanumeric serial. Older devices used a 12-character format that encoded factory and week-of-manufacture.

Where to find it

macOS: > About This Mac. iOS: Settings > General > About. Physical: bottom case (laptop), back (desktop), engraved on iPhone SIM tray (older models). From terminal: system_profiler SPHardwareDataType | grep Serial.

For business / GSX access

Apple Authorized Service Providers (AASP) and Apple Business Manager admins have access to GSX for fuller coverage and repair history.

Microsoft Surface

Serial Number & Coverage

Lookup URL

account.microsoft.com/devices — sign in with the Microsoft account the device is registered to and view registered devices + warranty. For commercial fleets, use Surface Management Portal through the Microsoft Intune admin center or contact Surface support with the serial.

Serial Number format

12-character alphanumeric. Example: 123456789012. Surface devices also have a separate IMEI on LTE models.

Where to find it

Surface app (preinstalled) > Your Surface tab — shows the serial. Or Settings > System > About. Physical: under the kickstand (Surface Pro), or in the SIM tray on LTE models. From CMD: wmic bios get serialnumber.

Commercial / bulk

Surface Management Portal in the Microsoft Intune admin center shows warranty across the fleet. Surface Enterprise Management Mode (SEMM) handles firmware controls.

When You Don't Have The Device

Pulling the serial remotely

Windows (any OEM)

CMD: wmic bios get serialnumber
PowerShell: Get-CimInstance Win32_BIOS | Select-Object SerialNumber, Manufacturer, Model

macOS

Terminal: system_profiler SPHardwareDataType | grep "Serial Number"

From Intune / SCCM / Jamf

All major MDMs report serial numbers per device. Export the device inventory and lookup in bulk.

From RMM

Datto, NinjaOne, ConnectWise Automate, N-able all expose Serial Number and Manufacturer fields per agent.

Quick Reference

One-line summary per vendor

Dell

7-char Service Tag (no I/O/L) → dell.com/support/warranty

Lenovo

7–8 char Serial + 4-char MTM → pcsupport.lenovo.com

HP

10-char Serial + Product Number → support.hp.com/us-en/checkwarranty

Apple

10-char (post-2021) or 12-char Serial → checkcoverage.apple.com

Microsoft Surface

12-digit Serial → account.microsoft.com/devices or Surface Management Portal in Intune
